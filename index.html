About Me

Hi, I'm Parsanna Koirala! I'm currently a junior at the University of Michigan Ann Arbor, majoring in Computer Science at the College of Engineering. I have a deep passion for technology and a curiosity for understanding how things work, which drives me to constantly learn and innovate.

I've had the opportunity to explore various domains within computer science, including machine learning, full-stack web development, and cloud computing. One of my most exciting projects was developing a deep convolutional neural network (DCNN) for lane detection in autonomous vehicles as part of the UM Autonomous Robotic Vehicle Team. I'm also gaining experience in leadership as a Building Supervisor at my college's gym, where I manage a team and oversee operations.

Beyond academics and work, I'm actively involved in my community, volunteering to help students learn new skills and support their growth. I'm always eager to meet new people, take on challenging projects, and make a positive impact through technology. In my free time, you might find me exploring new tech trends, reading, or enjoying a good workout.

I'm excited about the future and always looking for opportunities to grow, connect, and contribute to meaningful projects. Let's build something amazing together!
